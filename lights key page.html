What do the HUE Lights signify?

In conjunction to the game whenever it is Player 1's turn "X" on the left pane, Hue lights 1-3 will light up blue to represent the left players turn.

Which means whenever it is Player 2's turn "O" on the right pane, Hue lights 4-6 will light up blue to represent the right players turn. Rememeber to keep an eye out for the lights when you are playing the web application game on one of the local desktop machines in SMEATON 109.
